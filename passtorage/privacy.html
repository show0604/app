Privacy Policy

App Name: Passtorage

This application does not collect any personal data such as your name, email address, or phone number. All your sensitive data is stored locally on your device.

Your passwords and login information are stored securely in a local database on your device. We do not have access to your data. When you use the backup feature, the data is encrypted and saved to a file which you can then upload to a cloud service of your choice.
